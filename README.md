# Adobe Customer Journey Analytics (CJA) Portfolio

This repository contains a curated set of **Adobe Customer Journey Analytics (CJA)** projects demonstrating my ability to connect digital behavior to business outcomes through structured analysis and clear storytelling.

---

## 🎯 What This Portfolio Demonstrates

- Identify **drivers of conversion, cost, and customer behavior**
- Connect **digital interactions to downstream outcomes (calls, revenue, conversion)**
- Apply **attribution, segmentation, and journey analysis**
- Translate complex data into **clear, actionable business insights**

**Primary focus areas:**
- Call center optimization & digital self-service  
- Personalization & decisioning effectiveness  
- Cross-channel journey and attribution analysis

### 🔹 Personalization vs Fallback Performance Analysis  
**(Advanced — Personalization Effectiveness & Decisioning Evaluation)**

<img src="https://github.com/pgsmith2000/Adobe_CJA_Samples/blob/main/cja_personalization_strategy_analysis/cja_personalization_strategy_analysis.png" width="400"/>

**Executive Summary**  
Evaluates personalization effectiveness by separating **offer exposure (coverage)** from **outcome alignment (effectiveness)**. Introduces an Acceptance vs Rejection Index to normalize performance and compares strategies across both scale and impact.

Findings show that baseline user intent often outweighs personalization, with behavior-driven offers outperforming generic messaging while most strategies deliver incremental rather than transformative value.

**Business Impact:** Identifies which personalization strategies drive meaningful lift vs. noise.

### 🔹 AJO Journey Optimization: Engagement & Attribution  
**(Advanced — Attribution Modeling & Omni-Channel Analysis)**

<img src="https://github.com/pgsmith2000/Adobe_CJA_Samples/blob/main/ajo_journey_optimization_engagement/ajo_journey_optimization_engagement.png" width="400"/>

**Executive Summary**  
Analyzes journey performance using session and person-level attribution models to distinguish between **conversion drivers** and **influencers**. Highlights how journey impact evolves across sessions and channels.

Findings show abandonment journeys drive immediate conversion, while earlier-stage journeys influence downstream behavior.

**Business Impact:** Improves investment decisions across journey stages and channels.
